About This TODO App

A simple, fast, and reliable way to manage your daily tasks.

Our Mission

This TODO application was built to help individuals and teams stay organized without unnecessary complexity. Whether you're planning your day, tracking long-term goals, or managing a project, our app provides a clean and intuitive interface to add, update, and remove tasks effortlessly.

Built with Flask and SQLAlchemy on the backend, and styled with Bootstrap 5 on the frontend, this project demonstrates a full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) workflow in a lightweight, self-contained web application.

What You Can Do

📝
Simple Task Creation

Quickly add tasks with a title and description in just a few clicks.

✏️
Easy Editing

Update task details anytime as your plans change.

🗑️
Clean Removal

Delete completed or unwanted tasks to keep your list clutter-free.

Built With

Flask SQLAlchemy Bootstrap 5 SQLite Jinja2
